I am a qualified and experienced Integrative Psychotherapist. I offer a supportive, confidential therapy and have extensive experience working with people from diverse backgrounds with a wide range of different issues including relationship issues, low self -esteem, depression, mental health, substance misuse, anxiety, bereavement, divorce, eating disorders, feeling unfulfilled and many others.

Counselling and psychotherapy could help you to understand and gain a better awareness of yourself, enable to live a happier life and making authentic choices which can bring about lasting positive changes. Furthermore, it can enable you to find your answers from within, acknowledge and understand own feelings.

If you feel you could benefit from counselling, call or email me to set up an initial session where we can talk about what has lead you to counselling. If you will be happy to continue we will set up a second session, and will arrange to meet weekly or fortnightly.
